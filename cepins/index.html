CEPiNS: Conserved Exon Prediction in Novel Species

CEPiNS is a pipeline tool for mining exon boundaries of predicted gene sets from species with genomic sequence, and then using this information to identify the exon boundaries in the cDNA of a novel species through codon based alignment. CEPiNS provides an important comparative genomics tools necessary for developing SNPs, identified in transcriptome sequencing of novel or non-model species, that will work on genomic DNA. With extensive instructions and a test dataset, this pipeline is designed to be used by research labs with a range of bioinformatics backgrounds.
